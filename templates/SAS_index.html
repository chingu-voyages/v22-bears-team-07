Alt attributes

They say a picture is worth a thousand words, but imagine if there was no image.
Now what story are we trying to tell?
While some images are just for design and decoration, if an image tells a story, the alt attribute can assist those who don't have access to the image by telling them the story, the thousand words that may otherwise be unaccessible to them.
Click on the buttons above the images to hear the alt attributes.
